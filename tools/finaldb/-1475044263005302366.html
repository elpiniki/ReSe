Welcome to Sites.UDel.edu

A content management system using WordPress, hosted by Edublogs and supported by IT at UD.

Quick Answers to Your First Questions

Who is this service designed for?

  • Faculty who need a site for publicizing research work
  • Staff who support a web presence for their department or office
  • Registered student organizations
  • Current employees and students (subject to University policy)

What will the service provide?

  • Many themes ranging from simple to highly configurable
  • Support for mobile sites
  • Access to branding tools for UD
  • Storage for each site up to 10 GB
  • Built-in spam control sitewide for forms and comments
  • Training resources
  • Development consultation from IT-CS&S

What other UD options do I have?

For more options, contact the IT-Client Support & Services Web Presence team with questions or consultation requests.
