Advanced Studies – Educational Programs at IEC

 

 

The Institute is staffed by approximately 20 professional and support personnel, as well as faculty, visiting scholars, post-doctoral fellows, graduate, and undergraduate students. The broad scope of the research and development efforts offer opportunity for study in the fields of physics, chemistry, materials science, chemical engineering, mechanical engineering, and electrical engineering as well as collaboration with industrial groups seeking to manufacture and commercialize photovoltaics.

This section of the site describes the active educational program at the IEC, where both Graduate and Undergraduate students work with professional staff members on research projects or perform their dissertation work. You’ll also find more information of the Solar Energy Course at the University of Delaware, and the classes covering solar cells or solar electric systems.
