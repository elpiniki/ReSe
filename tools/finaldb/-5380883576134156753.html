Welcome to the Johns Hopkins University Integrated Photonics Laboratory

Our research involves the design, optimization and application of nanofabricated photonic devices for both linear and nonlinear optical interactions. We utilize CMOS-compatible fabrication techniques to implement our photonic devices in a manner that allows monolithic integration with electronics. These devices will serve as the basis for constructing compact chip-scale optical systems for a variety of functionalities including optical communications, sensing, imaging, spectroscopy, high speed processing and precision measurement.
