Climate & Energy Research Intern

The Worldwatch Institute has an opening for a full- or part-time Climate and Energy Research Intern to work on a range of international projects, including:

  • Low-carbon energy roadmaps for developing and emerging countries
  • Strategies and policies for transitioning the grid and increasing reliance on renewable power
  • Small-scale innovation for energy access in Africa

Approximately 30% of time will be dedicated to logistical support and 70% of time to research. Applicants should have a strong interest and background in energy and climate change issues. Language proficiency or experience in one of the geographical target areas (Caribbean, Central America, China, India and Africa) is a plus.

The 3-month long internship will begin September 2011.
