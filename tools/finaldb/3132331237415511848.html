JHU Office of Sustainability Releases Report, “Sustaining Change: Stories of Success”

The Johns Hopkins University (JHU) Office of Sustainability recently completed its report featuring stories on the projects, programs and progress toward making JHU a more sustainable institution. The report – which features campus performance data and highlighted stories from all JHU campuses and divisions – aims to show the variety and scope of sustainability action on campus. From operational improvement, to programming enhancement and additions, to student advocacy and of course, research, “Sustaining Change” helps reflect just how integrated sustainability is becoming as a principle and value throughout the entire organization. For a summary of E²SHI's work to-date, see page 18 of the report.
