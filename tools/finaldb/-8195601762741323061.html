Our research group focuses on various aspects (power, performance, area and reliability) of designing VLSI systems. We adopt a cross-cut level-transparent approach, where we jointly explore the design space of devices, circuits and architectures. Our group is affiliated with Boston University Photonics Center, NSF Center of Excellence for Learning in Education, Science and Technology and Boston University Center for Computational Neuroscience and Neural Technologies.
